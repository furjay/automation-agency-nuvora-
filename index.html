End‑to‑end AI Automation · Mid‑size SaaS · Kansas

Transforming high churn into loyal customers with a full AI revenue stack

A mid‑size SaaS company in Kansas was facing rising churn driven by poor customer support and scattered, manual revenue operations. Our audit uncovered slow responses, missed renewals, and money leaking across ad spend and invoicing.

We deployed a 24/7 AI support agent to handle customer queries, built smart money and invoice follow‑ups, added a potential‑client enricher, and designed an all‑in‑one sales infrastructure that scores leads, posts daily ads, and routes opportunities to the right reps.

To fix profitability, we implemented a custom AI “accountant” tuned to their model and a live dashboard that tracks the performance of the entire infrastructure in one place.

  • Lower churn by turning support into a 24/7 channel
  • Fewer leaks across invoicing and ad spend
  • Full visibility via a custom revenue dashboard
